About Forge

We built Forge because theme development should feel like design — not deciphering syntax.

Why we built Forge

Template engines are powerful, but they come with a learning curve. You have to remember the syntax for output tags, the right filter for each field type, the exact block structure for loops and conditionals.

Forge removes that friction entirely. You focus on the HTML — the design, the layout, the structure — and Forge handles the template syntax. It reads your HTML, understands what you're trying to do, and writes the tags for you.

Built by people who build themes

Forge was designed by the same team that builds Outpost CMS and ships themes every day.

Our values

Every decision we make comes back to these principles.

Developer experience first

If it takes more than three clicks, we haven't finished designing it. Every feature should feel obvious the first time you use it.

Zero lock-in

Your themes are HTML files. Your data is SQLite. Your images are files on disk. You can take everything and leave whenever you want.

Ship, don't spec

We release early, listen to feedback, and iterate fast. Forge went from idea to shipped feature in a single sprint.

Keep it simple

No Composer, no Docker, no build pipeline for the backend. Upload a folder, open the browser, start building. That's the whole setup.

Join the community

Forge is open to everyone building with Outpost. Come say hello and show us what you're making.
