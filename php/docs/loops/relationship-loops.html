Relationship Loops

Link collection items together with relationship fields. Editors hand-pick related items from any collection — the template receives full item data in the order they were selected, making it easy to build curated content connections.

What Is a Relationship Field?

Syntax

Use <outpost-each relationship="field_name"> to loop over a relationship field. Inside the loop, access item fields with data-outpost attributes.

<outpost-each relationship="field_name">
 <h3 data-outpost="title"></h3>
 <a data-outpost="url" data-type="link" href="#">View</a>
</outpost-each>

Related Posts Example 

<section class="related-posts">
 <h2>Related Posts</h2>
 <div class="related-grid">
 <outpost-each relationship="related_posts">
 <a class="related-card" data-outpost="url" data-type="link" href="#">
 <img data-outpost="featured_image" data-type="image" src="" alt="">
 <h3 data-outpost="title"></h3>
 <p data-outpost="excerpt"></p>
 </a>
 </outpost-each>
 </div>
</section>
